Built for networking.

Digital Contact Card, or simply the DC², is the new convenient way for tech savvy folks to connect and network. With the DC² you get the contact information of your choice in a sleek digital profile.

When you want to share your social media profiles with someone, simply touch your DC² to their phone's NFC sensor and voila, your contact information will be displayed on their phone's web browser.
